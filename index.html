What We Do Best

Aerial Construction: Building the Lines That Connect Communities

Our bread and butter is aerial construction — stringing coaxial and fiber optic cable on utility poles below the electric lines. This is skilled trade work. Our crews climb poles and operate bucket trucks to lash cable, make pole attachments, install hardware, and handle the underground transition where cable comes off the pole and goes into conduit.

If you need cable on poles done right — clean lashing, proper clearances, work that passes inspection — you need a crew that does this for a living. That's us.
