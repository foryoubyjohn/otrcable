Sarah Chen

Chief Technology Officer

Sarah Chen is a recognized expert in low voltage systems design and implementation, with a particular focus on scalable infrastructure solutions. As Chief Technology Officer, she leads OTR Cable Construction's technical strategy and ensures our solutions meet the highest standards of quality and innovation.

Professional Background

With over 15 years of experience in the telecommunications and low voltage industry, Sarah has designed and implemented complex infrastructure projects for healthcare facilities, educational institutions, and corporate headquarters. Her technical expertise includes fiber optic networks, wireless systems, and integrated building technologies.

Technical Expertise

Sarah specializes in designing future-proof infrastructure that can adapt to evolving technology needs. She is passionate about staying current with industry standards and emerging technologies, ensuring OTR Cable Construction remains at the forefront of the low voltage industry.
