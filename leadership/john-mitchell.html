John Mitchell

Chief Executive Officer

John Mitchell brings over 20 years of executive leadership experience in the technology and telecommunications industry to OTR Cable Construction. As Chief Executive Officer, he is responsible for setting the strategic direction of the company and ensuring we continue to deliver exceptional value to our clients.

Professional Background

Prior to joining OTR Cable Construction, John served as Vice President of Operations at a leading enterprise technology solutions provider, where he oversaw the implementation of large-scale infrastructure projects for Fortune 500 companies. His expertise spans structured cabling, data center design, and integrated security systems.

Education & Certifications

  • • Master of Business Administration, Technology Management
  • • Bachelor of Science, Electrical Engineering
  • • BICSI Registered Communications Distribution Designer (RCDD)
  • • PMP Certified Project Management Professional

Leadership Philosophy

John believes in building strong relationships with clients, partners, and team members. He emphasizes innovation, quality, and continuous improvement as the cornerstones of OTR Cable Construction's success. Under his leadership, the company has expanded its service offerings and maintained a reputation for excellence in the industry.
