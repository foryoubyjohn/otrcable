Control Access

Know Who's Where, When

Access control is about more than locking doors — it's about managing the flow of people through your facility with precision and accountability. We install systems that integrate with your security cameras, alarm systems, and building management platforms.

From a single door with a card reader to a multi-site enterprise deployment with hundreds of controlled points, we design and install access control that scales with your needs.

  • Card & Fob Readers — Proximity and smart card access points
  • Biometric Scanners — Fingerprint and facial recognition systems
  • Electric Strikes & Mag Locks — Door hardware and locking mechanisms
  • Cloud Management — Remote administration and credential management
  • Visitor Management — Guest registration and temporary access
