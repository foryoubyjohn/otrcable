## Competitor Services Analysis

### Ervin Cable Construction
Ervin Cable Construction is a full-service provider of broadband and telecom construction, including network design, and telecom engineering services. They offer turnkey services to multiple cable, energy, and communications companies, specializing in television receive-only installation, aerial & underground construction, and fiber splicing & engineering.

### Mason Technologies (Deer Park, NY)
Mason Technologies specializes in cabling, audio visual, data center, wireless network installation, and security solutions. They provide IT infrastructure solutions, including network design, installation, and maintenance, cable testing and certification, project management, system design, site survey, voice and data communications system relocation. They are a full-service integrator for AV, structured cabling, data centers, and unified security.

### LEK Technology Group (Prattville, AL)
LEK Technology Group is a full-service IT company specializing in structured cabling and large networking projects for K-12 schools, higher education, healthcare, and government facilities. Their services include structured cabling infrastructure, integrated wireless solutions, comprehensive physical security measures, audiovisual systems, data centers, and physical security. They design and install advanced low voltage systems, including structured cabling, security, access control, and audiovisual solutions.
